# LinkedIn Post Structure Templates
**Program:** LKD-MKT-2025-Q4
**Created:** November 2025
**Total Archetypes:** 5 proven post structures
**Purpose:** Standardize post creation for consistency and performance

---

## HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

**Step 1:** Choose archetype based on your content goal and pillar
**Step 2:** Fill in the structure with your specific content
**Step 3:** Optimize character count and formatting
**Step 4:** Add appropriate CTA based on desired action

**Key Principles:**
- **Hook within first 150 characters** (before "see more")
- **Line breaks every 1-2 sentences** (readability)
- **Bold or emoji for visual breaks** (scannable)
- **One clear CTA** (don't dilute focus)
- **2-3 hashtags max** (specific, not generic)

## ARCHETYPE 1: THE STORY POST

### Purpose
Build emotional connection, share lessons, humanize brand

### Best For
- Pillar 4 (Personal Stories)
- Pillar 7 (Behind-the-Scenes)
- Building trust and likability

### Optimal Length
800-1,200 characters (~150-200 words)

### Structure
```
[HOOK: Emotional or intriguing opening]

[SETUP: Context and stakes]

[CONFLICT: The challenge or turning point]

[RESOLUTION: What happened]

[LESSON: The takeaway or insight]

[CTA: Invitation to share similar experience]
```

### Character Count Guidelines
- Hook: 120-150 characters (must grab attention before "see more")
- Setup: 150-200 characters
- Conflict: 200-250 characters
- Resolution: 150-200 characters
- Lesson: 150-200 characters
- CTA: 50-100 characters

### Formatting Best Practices
✅ Start with dramatic hook
✅ Use line breaks between sections
✅ One emoji per section (optional)
✅ Bold key phrases for emphasis
✅ End with relatable question

## ARCHETYPE 2: THE TACTICAL POST

### Purpose
Provide step-by-step guidance, establish expertise, drive saves/shares

### Best For
- Pillar 1 (Tactical Education)
- Pillar 2 (Industry Insights - when sharing how-to)
- Driving actionable value

### Optimal Length
600-1,000 characters (~100-170 words)

### Structure
```
[HOOK: Promise specific outcome]

[CREDIBILITY: Why listen to you]

[STEPS: Numbered action items]
  Step 1: [Specific action]
  Step 2: [Specific action]
  Step 3: [Specific action]
  ...

[BONUS: Extra tip or common mistake]

[CTA: Ask about their biggest challenge]
```

### Character Count Guidelines
- Hook: 120-150 characters
- Credibility: 100-150 characters
- Each step: 80-120 characters
- Bonus: 100-150 characters
- CTA: 50-100 characters

### Formatting Best Practices
✅ Number your steps clearly
✅ Use → or bullets for sub-points
✅ Keep steps scannable (one action per step)
✅ Bold the action verb in each step
✅ Include "why" with "how"

---

## ARCHETYPE 3: THE DATA/INSIGHT POST

### Purpose
Establish authority, share original analysis, drive shares

### Best For
- Pillar 2 (Industry Insights)
- Pillar 5 (Customer Success - when sharing results)
- Positioning as thought leader

### Optimal Length
700-1,100 characters (~120-180 words)

### Structure
```
[HOOK: Surprising statistic or finding]

[METHOD: How you got the data]

[FINDINGS: Key insights]
  → Finding 1
  → Finding 2
  → Finding 3

[SO WHAT: What this means]

[ACTION: What to do about it]

[CTA: Ask if they're seeing same trend]
```

### Character Count Guidelines
- Hook: 120-150 characters
- Method: 100-150 characters
- Each finding: 100-150 characters
- So what: 150-200 characters
- Action: 150-200 characters
- CTA: 50-100 characters

### Formatting Best Practices
✅ Lead with most surprising data
✅ Use percentages and specific numbers
✅ Visual indicators (→ or •) for findings
✅ Explain implications (so what?)
✅ Cite source if external data

## ARCHETYPE 4: THE OPINION POST

### Purpose
Spark debate, differentiate brand, build memorable position

### Best For
- Pillar 3 (Thought Leadership)
- Building strong brand voice
- Generating high comment volume

### Optimal Length
500-900 characters (~80-150 words)

### Structure
```
[HOOK: Contrarian statement]

[ACKNOWLEDGE: Common belief]

[CHALLENGE: Why it's wrong]

[EVIDENCE: Why you're right]

[CALL TO ACTION: Invite debate]
```

### Character Count Guidelines
- Hook: 120-150 characters
- Acknowledge: 100-150 characters
- Challenge: 200-250 characters
- Evidence: 200-300 characters
- CTA: 50-100 characters

### Formatting Best Practices
✅ Be bold, not offensive
✅ Back opinion with reasoning
✅ Acknowledge other viewpoint
✅ Invite disagreement
✅ Prepare to defend in comments

---

→ Company stands alone eventually (equity value)

They built personal brands that ELEVATED company brands. Not replaced them.

#PersonalBrand #Entrepreneurship

**Why This Works:**
- Controversial take (personal brand hurts)
- Acknowledges common wisdom
- Provides specific reasoning
- Offers alternative approach
- Invites disagreement

*Example: "7 lessons from my biggest failure:"*

## FORMATTING RULES (APPLIES TO ALL ARCHETYPES)

### Character Limits
- **Optimal post length:** 800-1,200 characters
- **Minimum for depth:** 600 characters
- **Maximum before losing readers:** 1,500 characters

### Visual Breaks
- Line break every 1-2 sentences
- Emoji or bold text every 3-4 lines
- Numbered lists or bullets for scannability

### CTA Placement
- Always end with question or invitation
- Make it specific (not "thoughts?")
- Single CTA (don't ask for multiple actions)

### Hashtag Strategy
- 2-3 hashtags maximum
- Place at end of post
- Use specific tags (#B2BSales not #Success)
- Mix size: 1 large (100K+ followers), 2 niche (10K-50K)
