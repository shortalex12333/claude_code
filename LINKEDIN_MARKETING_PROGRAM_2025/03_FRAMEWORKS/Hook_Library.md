## HOOK TYPES & TEMPLATES

### üéØ CATEGORY 1: RESULTS & ACHIEVEMENT HOOKS
**Psychological Trigger:** Aspiration, Social Proof, Curiosity
**Best for Pillars:** 1 (Tactical Education), 5 (Customer Success)
**Avg. Engagement:** 5-7%

#### Templates:

**1.1 Specific Result Formula**
```
I [achieved X result] in [timeframe]. Here's how:
```
*Examples:*
- "I grew from 500 to 50K followers in 6 months. Here's how:"
- "We reduced churn from 12% to 2% in 90 days. Here's how:"
- "My client 3x'd their MRR in one quarter. Here's how:"

**Variables to optimize:**
- Result metric (be specific: %, $, number)
- Timeframe (shorter = more impressive)
- Add "without [common obstacle]" for extra impact

---

**1.2 Unexpected Achievement**
```
I just [surprising achievement] and I'm still processing it.
```
*Examples:*
- "I just turned down a $1M investment and I'm still processing it."
- "We just hit #1 on Product Hunt and I'm still processing it."
- "I just got acquired for 8 figures and I'm still processing it."

**Why it works:** Combines achievement with vulnerability and curiosity gap

---

**1.3 Before/After Transformation**
```
[Timeframe] ago, I was [negative state]. Today, I [positive state].
```
*Examples:*
- "2 years ago, I was unemployed. Today, I run a 7-figure agency."
- "6 months ago, we had 0 customers. Today, we have 500."
- "Last year, I was burned out. Today, I work 4 days a week and make more."

---

**1.4 Client Success Formula**
```
[Client name/type] just [achieved result]. Here's what we did:
```
*Examples:*
- "A SaaS founder just hit $100K MRR. Here's what we did:"
- "This marketing team 10x'd their pipeline. Here's what we did:"
- "Our client went from 0 to 50,000 users. Here's what we did:"

---

**1.5 Multi-Result Stacking**
```
In the last [timeframe], we [result 1], [result 2], and [result 3].
```
*Examples:*
- "In the last 90 days, we doubled revenue, hired 5 people, and launched 2 products."
- "This quarter, I grew followers by 20K, booked 50 demos, and closed $300K in deals."

## HOOK OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST

Before publishing, ensure your hook:

### ‚úÖ Specificity Check
- [ ] Uses specific numbers (not "many" or "most")
- [ ] Names specific groups/industries when relevant
- [ ] Includes concrete timeframes
- [ ] Avoids vague language

### ‚úÖ Curiosity Check
- [ ] Creates knowledge gap (promise of value)
- [ ] Doesn't give away the answer in hook
- [ ] Makes reader want to click "see more"
- [ ] Teases outcome without revealing method

### ‚úÖ Relevance Check
- [ ] Speaks directly to target persona
- [ ] Addresses actual pain point or aspiration
- [ ] Uses language audience uses
- [ ] Timely and current (not outdated)

### ‚úÖ Differentiation Check
- [ ] Not generic advice everyone shares
- [ ] Your unique perspective or data
- [ ] Different angle on common topic
- [ ] Memorable phrasing

## CREATING YOUR OWN HOOKS

**Formula for creating custom hooks:**

1. **Start with desired outcome:** What action do you want from reader?
2. **Identify psychological trigger:** Which trigger fits your content?
3. **Add specificity:** Numbers, names, exact details
4. **Create curiosity gap:** Promise value without revealing all
5. **Test and track:** Measure performance, iterate

**Example Creation Process:**
- Desired outcome: Get comments on new feature
- Trigger: Curiosity + Social proof
- Draft: "We just launched [feature]. Here's the response:"
- Add specificity: "We just launched AI scheduling. 87% of beta users called it 'magic':"
- Create gap: "We just launched AI scheduling. 87% of beta users called it 'magic'. The surprising part:"
