# LinkedIn Content Taxonomy Framework
**Program:** LKD-MKT-2025-Q4
**Created:** November 2025
**Purpose:** Strategic content pillar system for LinkedIn organic growth
**Based on:** Algorithm research, competitor analysis, audience personas, B2B thought leadership best practices

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This content taxonomy defines **7 strategic pillars** that will guide all LinkedIn content creation for the next 90 days and beyond. Each pillar serves a specific purpose in the customer journey, appeals to different audience personas, and is optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm preferences.

**Strategic Distribution:**
- **Primary Pillars (60% of content):** Tactical Education, Industry Insights
- **Secondary Pillars (30% of content):** Personal Stories, Thought Leadership, Customer Success
- **Supporting Pillars (10% of content):** Community Building, Behind-the-Scenes
