### The Algorithm Advantage

**LinkedIn's 2025 Algorithm Prioritizes:**

1. **Early Engagement (First Hour)**
   - First 60 minutes determine distribution
   - Reply to all comments immediately
   - Seed engagement with close connections

2. **Quality over Quantity (Comments > Likes)**
   - 50 thoughtful comments > 500 likes
   - Algorithm detects engagement depth
   - Specific CTAs drive comments

3. **Topic Authority (Consistent Niche Focus)**
   - Posting on same topics builds authority
   - Algorithm identifies experts
   - Niche content outperforms generic

4. **Native Content (No External Links)**
   - Outbound links penalized (-50% reach)
   - Put links in first comment
   - Keep content on LinkedIn

5. **Dwell Time (Longer Reading = Higher Ranking)**
   - 800-1200 character sweet spot
   - Line breaks every 1-2 sentences
   - "See more" click increases dwell time

**Our Strategy Leverages All 5**
